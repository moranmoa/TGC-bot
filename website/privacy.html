Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services (such as dynamic voice channels and spam protection), TGC-ChatBOT may collect and temporarily store the following information:

  • Discord User IDs and Usernames
  • Server (Guild) IDs and Channel IDs
  • Message content (strictly for real-time spam filtering; messages are not permanently stored)
  • Voice channel states (when joining/leaving channels)

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used solely for the core functionality of the bot:

  • Creating and managing temporary voice channels based on your activity.
  • Analyzing message patterns to detect and mitigate spam and malicious behavior.
  • Troubleshooting technical issues and improving bot performance.

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data such as user IDs and server settings are stored securely. Message content is processed in memory for spam detection and is discarded immediately after analysis. We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

Server administrators can remove the bot at any time, which stops all data collection for that server. If you wish to request the deletion of any stored configuration data related to your server, please contact us via our Support server.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us on our Discord Support Server.
